Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion & Unity

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit licensed by the Central Government — ISO 9001:2015 certified, 80G & 12A approved, and NITI Aayog registered.

In a world that often feels divided, InAmigos stands as a beacon of hope — dedicated to unity and support within society. We address the needs of underserved communities through education, food distribution, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental protection, and skill development. Our national network of dedicated professionals and volunteers works tirelessly to bring positive, lasting change across India.
